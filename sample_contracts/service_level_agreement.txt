SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)

Effective Date: February 1, 2024
Between: Tech Solutions Ltd. ("Service Provider") and Enterprise Corp. ("Client")

1. SERVICE DESCRIPTION
Service Provider will provide cloud infrastructure services including:
- 24/7 server monitoring and support
- Regular backups and disaster recovery
- Security patches and updates
- Performance optimization

2. SERVICE AVAILABILITY
2.1 Uptime Guarantee: Service Provider guarantees 99.9% uptime excluding scheduled maintenance.
2.2 Scheduled Maintenance: Up to 4 hours per month during off-peak hours (2 AM - 6 AM UTC).
2.3 Uptime Measurement: Measured on a monthly basis.

3. RESPONSE TIME COMMITITIES
3.1 Critical Issues: Response within 30 minutes, resolution target 4 hours
3.2 High Priority: Response within 2 hours, resolution target 24 hours
3.3 Medium Priority: Response within 8 hours, resolution target 48 hours
3.4 Low Priority: Response within 24 hours, resolution target 5 business days

4. SERVICE CREDITS
If Service Provider fails to meet SLA targets:
- 95-99.9% availability: 5% monthly fee credit
- 90-95% availability: 10% monthly fee credit
- Below 90% availability: 25% monthly fee credit or termination option

5. EXCLUSIONS
Downtime caused by:
- Client's network or hardware failures
- Denial of service attacks
- Force majeure events
- Client-caused misconfigurations

6. MONITORING AND REPORTING
6.1 Service Provider will maintain real-time monitoring dashboards accessible to Client.
6.2 Monthly performance reports will be provided by the 5th of each month.
6.3 Quarterly review meetings will be scheduled to discuss performance metrics.

7. SUPPORT ESCALATION
- Level 1: Standard support team
- Level 2: Senior technical team
- Level 3: Engineering team lead
- Level 4: Vice President of Operations

8. TERM AND TERMINATION
This SLA remains in effect for the duration of the service contract. Either party may terminate with 30 days notice.
