MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (NDA)

Effective Date: January 10, 2024
Between: TechVenture Partners LLC ("Disclosing Party") and Innovation Labs Inc. ("Receiving Party")

1. PURPOSE
This NDA is entered into to facilitate discussion and evaluation of potential business opportunities between the parties.

2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
Confidential Information includes but is not limited to:
- Business plans and strategies
- Financial information and projections
- Technical data and source code
- Customer lists and contact information
- Proprietary processes and methodologies
- Product roadmaps and development plans
- Research and development work

3. OBLIGATIONS
3.1 The Receiving Party agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of all Confidential Information and limit disclosure to employees with a need to know.
3.2 The Receiving Party shall not disclose Confidential Information to third parties without written consent.

4. EXCEPTIONS
Confidential Information excludes information that:
- Is or becomes publicly available through no breach
- Is already in possession of Receiving Party prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed without use of Confidential Information

5. DURATION
The obligations under this NDA shall remain in effect for 3 years from the Effective Date.

6. RETURN OF INFORMATION
Upon request or termination, Receiving Party shall return or certify destruction of all Confidential Information.

7. NO LICENSE GRANTED
This NDA does not grant any license, right, or interest in any Confidential Information.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of New York.
