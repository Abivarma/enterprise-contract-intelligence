VENDOR AGREEMENT

This Vendor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2024 ("Effective Date") between Acme Corporation, a Delaware corporation ("Client"), and Global Supply Inc., a California corporation ("Vendor").

1. SERVICES
Vendor agrees to provide software development and maintenance services as outlined in Exhibit A, including:
- Custom software development
- System integration
- Technical support and maintenance
- Performance monitoring

2. TERM
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of 12 months, unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 6.

3. COMPENSATION
3.1 Service Fees: Client agrees to pay Vendor $50,000 per month for the services described above.
3.2 Payment Terms: Invoice will be issued monthly, due within 30 days of receipt.
3.3 Late Payment: Payments not received by the due date shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
4.1 All work product created by Vendor shall be the exclusive property of Client.
4.2 Vendor retains rights to pre-existing materials and tools used in performance of services.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary information disclosed during the term of this Agreement and for 2 years thereafter.

6. TERMINATION
6.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.
6.2 Upon termination, all outstanding invoices become immediately due.

7. LIABILITY LIMITATION
Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages exceeding the total fees paid in the preceding 12 months.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
